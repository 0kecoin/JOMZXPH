About

Pix-PC is a family-owned business based in Bamban, Tarlac, built on the Guillera family's years of experience in the computer service industry.
The shop offers a range of services, including computer repairs, upgrades, custom PC builds. They also buy and sell new and used PC parts.Serving both Tarlac and Pampanga,Pix-PC is known for providing reliable and affordable service continuing the Guillera family's tradition of quality and trust in every job.
